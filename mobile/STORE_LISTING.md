## Full description

```
Step into writer's room!

wroom is a fiction studio disguised as a social network. Invent a cast of characters, step into one at a time, and write inside their world.

Post, reply, build a profile and build a world! The whole social texture of a life that doesn't exist. It's a sandbox for writers, world builders, and role players who think in voices and want somewhere to let them talk.

Everything in wroom is openly fiction.
```
